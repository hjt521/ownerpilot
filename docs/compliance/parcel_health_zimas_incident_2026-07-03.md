## §3 — Root-cause note (not blocking)

`zimas` (LA City ZIMAS parcel viewer, `zimas.lacity.org`) has historical intermittent availability characteristic of city-hosted GIS infrastructure — planned maintenance windows, load-balancer/CDN adjacencies, and occasional CivicPlus-adjacent outages. A 90-minute transient with clean recovery and zero consecutive failures post-recovery is consistent with a typical LA City infra blip, NOT with a persistent degradation, endpoint deprecation, or breaking API change.

Because the outage was `http_status`-classed (not `content_shape` or `latency`), it is almost certainly server-side unavailability rather than a data-shape drift that would require us to re-verify the ZIMAS adapter (per `la_geocode_zimas_adapter_diagnostic_v6_broker_ratification_2026-06-20.md`).

**No adapter change, no ruling change, no cron change.** Standing.

If none of the above trigger, this incident is closed at first successful re-probe (which has already occurred at 20:00:03Z).

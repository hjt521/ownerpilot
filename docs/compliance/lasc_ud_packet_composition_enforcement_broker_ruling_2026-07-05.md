## §1 — The gap

### §1.1 What the SSOT already says

`ud_complaint_filing_packet_scope_broker_ruling_2026-06-30_supersession.md` §4 enumerated 7 forms as the LASC UD packet composition:

| # | Form | Issuer | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | CM-010 | Judicial Council | Civil Case Cover Sheet |
| 2 | LACIV-109 | LASC | Civil Case Cover Sheet Addendum + Statement of Location |
| 3 | UD-100 | Judicial Council | Complaint — Unlawful Detainer |
| 4 | **CP-10.5** | Judicial Council | **Prejudgment Claim of Right of Possession** |
| 5 | SUM-130 | Judicial Council | Summons — UD / Eviction |
| 6 | POS-010 | Judicial Council | Proof of Service of Summons |
| 7 | **LASC CIV 312** | LASC | **UD Cellular Telephone Information** |

### §1.2 What the as-built script actually produced

The Clifton Alexander combined packet PDF filed 2026-07-02 contained: CM-010, LACIV-109, UD-100, SUM-130 (4 of 7). **CP-10.5 and LASC CIV 312 were not generated.** POS-010 was correctly excluded from the initial filing packet (POS-010 is filed separately post-service per §3.2 below); it is not one of the two missing forms.

### §1.3 Why this matters

**CP-10.5 (Prejudgment Claim of Right of Possession).** Without CP-10.5 served alongside the SUM-130 at the tenant's premises, an unnamed adult occupant retains the right to intervene later and claim possession — restarting the eviction against that occupant from scratch. LASC accepts UD complaints without CP-10.5, but the resulting judgment is not enforceable against unnamed occupants. For any premises where the operator cannot personally confirm every adult on-site is a named defendant, CP-10.5 is not optional. Given OwnerPilot's intake does not currently gate the packet on operator-confirmed occupant enumeration, **default: include CP-10.5**.

**LASC CIV 312 (UD Cellular Telephone Information).** Los Angeles Superior local mandatory form disclosing the plaintiff's cell number for court contact. LASC has been enforcing this locally as a filing requirement. Missing it leads to counter delay or rejection.

Both forms are on the official Stanley Mosk handout the operator picked up 2026-07-05 (Doc-18.pdf). They are unambiguously mandatory.

## §2 — Ruling

### §2.1 Composition — reaffirmed as mandatory

The LASC UD filing packet, as produced by OwnerPilot's assembly script, MUST include all seven forms enumerated in §1.1. This is not broker-discretion. This is not opt-in. This is the packet.

**Exceptions (narrow):**

- **POS-010** is generated as a standalone form to be filed *after* service on the tenant. It is NOT included in the initial-filing bundle. This exception was already correct in the as-built script.
- **CP-10.5** may be omitted only if the operator affirmatively confirms at intake that every adult occupant at the premises is a named defendant on UD-100 AND acknowledges the trade-off. Default state: included.
- **All other forms** (CM-010, LACIV-109, UD-100, SUM-130, LASC CIV 312): mandatory, no exceptions, no operator override.

### §2.2 Assembly-script fix

Engineering MUST amend `rebuild_combined_packet.py` (or the successor packet-assembly module) to:

1. **Generate CP-10.5** from Judicial Council fillable template, pre-fill with premises address + landlord name + case caption (case number is [BROKER-INPUT] until file-stamped).
2. **Generate LASC CIV 312** from the LASC local fillable template, pre-fill with plaintiff name + plaintiff cell number ([BROKER-INPUT] at intake — the operator's cell, not the tenant's).
3. **Insert both forms in the combined packet PDF** in the LASC packet order: CM-010 → LACIV-109 → UD-100 → CP-10.5 → SUM-130 → LASC CIV 312 (POS-010 stays separate, downstream artifact).
4. **Verify page count** — the combined-packet PDF grows by however many pages CP-10.5 + LASC CIV 312 total (CP-10.5 is 2 pages; LASC CIV 312 is 1 page; net +3 pages).
5. **Update `clifton_alexander_packet_manifest_2026-07-02.md` template** — but do NOT run the update on the already-filed Clifton packet. Reserve for the next packet.

### §2.4 Cell-number field — intake requirement

LASC CIV 312 requires the plaintiff's cell phone number. This is a **new intake field** OwnerPilot must collect before the UD packet can be generated. Requirements:

- Intake label: "Cell number for court contact (LASC local rule — mandatory)"
- Storage: encrypted at rest, retention tied to case lifecycle
- Not shared with tenants, not printed on tenant-serve copies of the summons
- Included only on the LASC CIV 312 sheet
- [BROKER-INPUT] flag if operator declines to provide → block packet generation with disclosure: "LASC CIV 312 requires this field; the packet cannot be assembled without it"

## §3 — 3-Copies clerk procedure — reinforcement

### §3.1 Reaffirmation

The operator's first-hand observation from Stanley Mosk Courthouse remains the authoritative procedural guidance:

> "The court clerk told me to have 3 copies of all the docs, the clerk stamped all 3 with the file number and date, kept 2 copies and gave me 1 copy back."

This is captured verbatim in `lasc_ud_filing_three_copies_procedural_ruling_2026-07-05.md` §"The operator's first-hand observation" and codified as a persona paragraph in that ruling's §"Persona reference context".

### §3.2 What gets brought to the counter (three copies each)

Per `lasc_ud_filing_three_copies_procedural_ruling_2026-07-05.md` §"The correct procedural guidance", updated here to reflect the mandatory 7-form composition:

Original + two copies of every form in the packet:

- CM-010 (Civil Case Cover Sheet) ×3
- LACIV-109 (Civil Case Cover Sheet Addendum + Statement of Location) ×3
- UD-100 (Complaint — Unlawful Detainer) ×3
- CP-10.5 (Prejudgment Claim of Right of Possession) ×3
- SUM-130 (Summons — Eviction) ×3
- LASC CIV 312 (UD Cellular Telephone Information) ×3
- Attached exhibits (3-day notice, proof of service of 3-day notice, lease, LAHD Eviction Filing Cover Sheet, LAHD RTC Notice EN + ES) ×3

**Clerk workflow:** stamps all three copies with filing date + assigned case number → keeps two (court file + service copy) → returns one conformed copy to operator.

**POS-010 is not brought to the initial filing.** POS-010 is completed after the summons + complaint are served on the tenant by a registered process server (or non-party over 18), and filed separately with the court.

### §3.3 Packet walkthrough page — mandatory content

Every UD packet OwnerPilot generates MUST include, at or near the end of Section 2 (before exhibits in Section 3), a **"LASC Filing Counter Walkthrough"** page reproducing the 3-copies discipline in plain English. Content specification per `lasc_ud_filing_three_copies_procedural_ruling_2026-07-05.md` §"Packet-assembly walkthrough copy":

1. Header: "LASC Filing Counter Walkthrough — In-Person UD Filing"
2. Three-copies rule: verbatim from the persona paragraph
3. What to bring: enumerated list of every form in the packet, marked **×3 each**
4. What the clerk does: stamps all three, keeps two, returns one
5. What operator walks out with: one conformed copy — evidence of filing + copy that gets served
6. What NOT to file at this visit: POS-010 (comes later, after tenant is served)
7. Filing fees: current amount + FW-001 fee-waiver pointer
8. Branch mapping: [BROKER-INPUT] branch already on the packet cover
9. After the counter: registered process server → serve tenant → complete POS-010 → file POS-010 separately

**Insertion point in the combined packet PDF:** After the LASC CIV 312 form (last UD-100 packet form) and before the Section 3 (exhibits) divider.

## §4 — Filing fees — reaffirmed

Per the Stanley Mosk handout (Doc-18.pdf) and prior rulings:

- $240 — complaint demanding $12,500 or less (limited civil)
- $385 — complaint demanding $12,500 to $35,000 (limited civil)
- $435 — complaint demanding $35,000 or more (unlimited civil)

**Fee-waiver path:** Judicial Council form FW-001. Operators who qualify may file for fee waiver alongside the complaint. OwnerPilot should surface FW-001 at intake as a checkbox option; not opt-out, not upsell.

**Filing address (Central District):** Stanley Mosk Courthouse, 111 N. Hill Street, Los Angeles CA 90012, Attention: Filing Window Room 102. Self-addressed stamped envelope required for the returned conformed copy if filing by mail.

**Verification cadence:** LASC filing fees have changed twice in the last 5 years. OwnerPilot's statute/fee watch discipline should include a periodic check of the LASC current fee schedule ([`https://www.lacourt.org/`](https://www.lacourt.org/)) — quarterly cadence at minimum. Consider adding this to the standing crons landscape as `LASC_FEE_SCHEDULE_WATCH`.

# CK-001 — Constitutional Query Engine™ (PROPOSED)

> **Recorded, not designed.** The Founder recommended CK-001 as the single foundational capability to introduce **once P5.5 (Knowledge Graph) is complete** — a stronger next-generation foundation than another standalone intelligence model. This file records the specification; design is **not authorized** and must wait until its dependencies (Library, Graph, Registry, Index) exist.

## Purpose
The primary way both humans and AI **navigate the constitutional platform** — a query layer over the Knowledge Library, Knowledge Graph, Intelligence Model Registry, and Constitutional Index. It answers governance questions directly instead of requiring manual repository archaeology.

## Representative queries (Founder spec)
- "Which ADR governs this capability?"
- "Which Enterprise Architecture documents affect TM-001?"
- "What Founder ratification authorized this standard?"
- "Show every artifact that depends on EA-012."
- "What changed between Constitution v1.1 and v1.2?"

## Why after P5.5 (sequencing rationale)
CK-001 is only as good as the structures it queries: it needs the **Index** (stable IDs), the **Knowledge Library** (content), the **Knowledge Graph** (relationships/dependencies), and the **Registry** (models). Building it before those exist would force it to invent its own inventory. Introducing it *right after* P5.5 gives every later intelligence model (and CA-001) a shared navigation/traceability surface to build on — hence "foundational capability, not domain intelligence."

## Constitutional posture (inherited)
Read/navigate/explain only — CK-001 **answers**, it does not ratify, mutate, or make adverse decisions. Answers are evidence-backed and cite the canonical artifacts they draw from (never fabricated references); `Indeterminate`/"not found" when the structures don't contain the answer. Governed by EA-000 (map) and EA-012 (intelligence-model rules); audited by CA-001.

## Canonical placement intent (mapping is post-P5.5 work; NOT done here)
Likely a capability registered in `constitution.capabilities` that reads the Index + Graph + Registry; **no new authoritative data of its own** (it queries existing canonical sources). Resolved during design, after P5.5.
